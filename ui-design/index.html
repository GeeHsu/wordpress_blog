發表於2020 年 1 月 25 日 由gee.hsu撰寫

Understanding & Applying Design Fundamentals

What is UI Design?

A digital medium that a user interacts with.

UI vs UX Design

UI is visual (screens, pages, visual elements) and UX is focused on the user’s experience while they’re interacting with your product or service.

“UX is focused on the user’s journey to solve a problem, UI is focused on how a product’s surfaces look and function”
– Ken Norton (Partner at Google Ventures)

“UI is the bridge that gets us where we want to go, UX is the feeling we get when we arrive.”
– Jason Ogle (Host & Producer of User Defenders Podcast)

“A UX designer is concerned with the conceptual aspects of the design process, leaving the UI designer to focus on the more tangible elements.”
– Andy Budd(Co-founder of Clearleft)

UI Design vs. Frontend Developer

A UI Designer handles designing and prototyping the screen(s), while a frontend developer takes those designs and uses HTML, CSS & JavaScript to make them work on device

Designing & Prototyping UI’s

Choose your weapon of choice
-Sketch
-Adobe XD
-Figma
-Invision
-Gravit Designer
-Framer

Design Fundamentals

  • Color & Contrast (顏色 & 對比度)
  • White Space (空白空間)
  • Visual Hierarchy (視覺層次)
  • Complexity vs. Simplicity (複雜 vs. 簡單)
  • Consistency (一致性)
  • Scale (大小)
